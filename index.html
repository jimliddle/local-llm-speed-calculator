The model:

  • Bytes/token = active_params × bits/8 + KV_cache
  • KV is read in full every token (dense or MoE) and pinned to primary memory.
  • Weights fill primary, then spill to RAM, then SSD; each tier read at its own bandwidth.
  • Speed = 1 ÷ (weight_read_time + kv_read_time)

Ignores prefill, compute on tiny models, and software overhead. An optimistic ceiling; treat as best-case.
